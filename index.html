Intermission

Starting out as a young kid with wits, I got lured into the pursuit of a career which I'm still very proud of but I might not have chosen it over this new course. My flare for computer and anything tech almost won me another college but it didn't.

An internship with an Agro-tech start-up company, El-Kanis & Partners where I worked with seasoned and beginner tech guys got me informed, exposed and initiated into the technology world. This eventually precipitated into a premise which would forever struggle with my choice, leaving a tiny hope for indifference.

Furthermore, my ineptitude in web development proved to have sabotaged lots of my productive intuitions and possible inventions for the emerging era that is being starved of equity-focused opportunities for career development, optimum participation in education and lifechanging empowerments for the youth.

At this point, I knew I had to do something! I had wanted to build an online preparatory school that would prepare kids for qualifying examinations in the face of Covid-19, also to launch a platform that equips the younger generation with soft skills that will enhance their employability skills or inspire their independent ingenuity for optimum self actualization while rendering service to the world of jobs.
